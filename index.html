Thank you for visiting my online resume and portfolio. Hi, I am Josh. I am a designer/developer. I love to create applications for the web that are both visually stunning and function flawlessly. I recently finished a development bootcamp at The Iron Yard in Atlanta, GA. Here i have learned the ins and outs of front end web development. with the previous 10 + years of experience with adobe design products and working on design projects for clients ranging from web to print, I am able to provide clients a product that is on the cutting edge of their marketplace.

Please feel free to get in touch with me via email or phone. I am available to take your call and I am looking forward to joining your team.

DESIGN

Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Lightroom, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe After Effects,

DEVELOPMENT

HTML5, CSS3, SASS, Bourbon, Neat, Bitters, Refills, Yeoman, Gulp, Bower, NPM, Node, JavaScript, JQuery, Underscore, Backbone, Parse, Heroku, Chai, Mocha, React.js, Aviary SDK, Ampersand.js "The Flux Model"

WORK-FLOW

GIT, GitHub, Invision, Trello, AGILE, Pivitol Tracker, Slack, HipChat

Design

I Have been a designer for 10 plus years. I have played an integral part in designing websites, print media, Video editing and animation design, audio engineering and sound design, logo design and branding. I have worked for myself as well as for medium to large sized companies. I stay up to date with trends in design as well as try to anticipate the next awesome style. I Love design. I tend to work in excess.Check out my Portfolio to see examples of work i have done recently.

Development

I Build. I love to create and problem-solve. I have always been driven to figure out how to accomplish things with little or no direction, even though I do welcome structure and standards. I develop robust,rich applications utilizing the most up to date technology and tools to deliver the best performance with a clean interface and engaging user experience. With the emergence of mobile devices, developers have to create experiences that suit the device the user is connected through. I pay strong attention to what is going on in the world of mobile technology and make all of my applications/websites viewable on all devices. I have been taught to not only write great applications, but also test them in a fashion which other team members can refactor with minimal pain. I have been taught to unit test. Please see examples of my applications in my portfolio or on GitHub https://github.com/joshdaniels

Work-Flow

Using GIT and Github has changed the way I approach projects. From the client being able to see the development process, to the team working together as a well-oiled machine.GitHub has given depth and structure to my development workflow. I dont know what i would do without some form of version control. I use GIT from begining to end with design, prototyping and development. Check out my GitHub Open Source Report Card. Testing has allowed my applications to be refactorable and easy for other people to navigate. I know when something breaks and where in my application the problem is. I use Chai and Mocha to unit test my applications.
